# Synthetic Daily Market Data

These CSV files contain deterministic synthetic records for local development
and automated tests. They are not current or historical market statements.

- Price adjustment: unadjusted synthetic prices.
- Price and amount currency: synthetic CNY-denominated values.
- Volume unit: shares.
- Source identifier to use when importing: `synthetic_csv_v1`.
- Intended use: research-platform development and education only.

The sample does not contain recommendations, brokerage data, account data, or
trade instructions.
